<Meta title="Challenges of responsive design/Fluid layouts" />

And as time went on, the line between mobile and desktop layouts became blurrier.

Different parts of the design would resize or reposition at different breakpoints, and some parts of the design even became fluid.

We stopped talking about mobile and desktop versions, and started the trends known as fluid layouts and responsive design.
